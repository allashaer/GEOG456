Observation Patterns in NC: Using Citizen Science for Conservation

This website is a composition of North Carolina flora observations in iNaturalist from 2015-2025. The purpose of this project is to create awareness for the importance of citizen science, and encourage our users to engage with, record, and verify flora sightings in nature.

The research-grade category is assigned to observations that are verified by another user in iNaturalist. The unverified category is assigned to observations that the uploader self-verified but has not been approved by a second user.
