Downtown Durham, NC

Aerial changes displayed from 2010-2022

From 2010 to 2016, the new Durham County Courthouse seen in the top left has completed construction. The car dealership at the bottom is in the process of being redeveloped. The Durham Country Department of Public Health seen in the top right has been redeveloped. From 2016 to 2022, the development project to turn the previous car dealership into luxury condos and apartments is completed. In 2022, the surface lot on the left is in the process of being redeveloped.
